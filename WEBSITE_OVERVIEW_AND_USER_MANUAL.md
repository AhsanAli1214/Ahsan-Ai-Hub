## üìñ USER MANUAL

### **Step 1: Access & Setup**
1. Visit: `https://ahsan-ai-hub.vercel.app`
2. **Install**: Scroll to the "Official App" section and click "Install" to add to your Home Screen.
3. **Notify**: Enable Push Notifications to get updates on new AI features.

### **Step 2: Using the AI Studio**
1. **Chat**: Use the "AI Chat" page. Click the **Camera** icon for Vision tasks or the **Microphone** icon for Voice Chat.
2. **Solve Math**: Go to "Content Tools" > "Math Solver". You can upload an image of your problem for a step-by-step solution.
3. **Refine Settings**: Go to "Settings" to pick your favorite color theme (Blue, Purple, Teal, etc.) and set your AI's personality.

### **Step 3: Managing Privacy**
1. **Enable Biometric Lock**: In Settings, turn on the Biometric switch to protect your history.
2. **Clear History**: Use the "Delete All" button in the History tab if you want to wipe your local records instantly.
3. **Export Data**: Download your entire interaction history as a file for backup.
